## Purpose

Build a deep, evidence-graded and app-ready knowledge base for Moolank 1. The system must preserve traditional numerology interpretations while clearly separating them from psychological concepts and scientific evidence.

This document defines the full research boundary. It is not a final personality profile and must not be shown to users as deterministic truth.

## Core calculation rule

Moolank is calculated from the calendar day of birth only.

- 1 → 1
- 10 → 1 + 0 = 1
- 19 → 1 + 9 = 10 → 1 + 0 = 1
- 28 → 2 + 8 = 10 → 1 + 0 = 1

The dates 1, 10, 19 and 28 form the Moolank 1 date family. Compound-date interpretations must remain separate from the shared root-number profile.

## Evidence-grading framework

| Grade | Meaning |
|---|---|
| T1 | Claim appears in one significant traditional source |
| T2 | Claim appears in two or more independent traditional sources |
| T3 | Broad agreement across multiple numerology schools |
| C1 | Cultural or symbolic association |
| P1 | A related psychological concept exists, but numerology linkage is unproven |
| E0 | No established numerology-specific empirical evidence |
| X1 | Conflicting traditional interpretations |
| R1 | Risky claim requiring restriction or strong qualification |
| P0 | Prohibited for app use |

## Non-negotiable safety rules

- Numerology claims are not scientific facts.
- Birth date alone cannot diagnose personality, health, mental illness, criminality, addiction or relationship quality.
- Avoid “you are” wording where evidence supports only a traditional or conditional theme.
- No fatalistic, fear-based or guaranteed predictions.
- No medical or psychiatric treatment advice derived from numerology.
- No fixed negative labels such as narcissistic, toxic, abusive, unfaithful or unsuccessful.
- User-facing outputs should encourage reflection, agency and revision.
