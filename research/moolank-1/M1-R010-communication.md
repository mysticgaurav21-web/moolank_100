# M1-R010 — Communication Deep Dive

## Metadata
- Record ID: M1-R010
- Status: Research complete; canonical extraction approved

## Dimensions
Directness, clarity, listening, turn-taking, emotional tone, disagreement, feedback, apology, persuasion, negotiation and boundary communication.

## Expression model
**Balanced:** states needs clearly, distinguishes facts from interpretations, listens to completion, adapts tone to context, asks before advising, and repairs impact when intent and impact differ.

**Overextended:** uses commands where requests are appropriate, interrupts, compresses complexity, treats disagreement as obstruction, gives unsolicited solutions, or prioritizes winning over understanding.

**Underexpressed:** withholds needs, softens messages until meaning is lost, agrees publicly but resists privately, avoids feedback, or delays necessary conversations.

## High-value practices
- Observation–impact–request format
- Two-minute listening without interruption
- Summarize the other view before rebuttal
- Separate urgent from important
- Ask: “Do you want listening, ideas or a decision?”
- Repair: acknowledge impact, clarify responsibility, propose change

## Triggers
Time pressure, perceived incompetence, repeated misunderstanding, public challenge, slow discussion and unclear ownership.

## App-safe prompts
- Was your sentence a request, command or assumption?
- What did the other person actually say?
- How might your tone be heard by someone with less power?
- What repair is needed even if your intention was good?

## Safety
Do not label users aggressive, abusive, manipulative or emotionally unavailable from birth date. Relationship safety concerns require behaviour-specific assessment.

## Growth target
Clear and collaborative communication: direct without domination, receptive without self-erasure.
