# M1-R013 — Relationships Deep Dive

## Metadata
- Record ID: M1-R013
- Status: Research complete; canonical extraction approved

## Dimensions
Closeness, autonomy, reciprocity, trust, affection, support, conflict, apology, repair, boundaries, shared decisions and appreciation.

## Balanced expression
Maintains individuality while investing in mutuality; communicates needs directly; respects a partner’s agency; offers practical support; receives influence; repairs conflict; appreciates contribution; and negotiates shared decisions.

## Under pressure
May over-direct, interpret requests as control, avoid vulnerable dependence, compete over who is right, seek respect through authority, or withdraw after feeling unappreciated.

## Underexpressed pattern
May suppress preferences to avoid conflict, over-adapt to a stronger partner, hesitate to ask for reassurance, or remain silent until resentment builds.

## Protective factors
Explicit agreements, equal speaking time, separate and shared goals, appreciation rituals, conflict time-outs, repair conversations and consent-based advice.

## Reflection prompts
- Are you asking for cooperation or compliance?
- Can both people retain dignity and choice?
- What need is hidden beneath the argument?
- What repair would rebuild trust?

## Compatibility boundary
No pairing is guaranteed compatible or incompatible. Moolank pairings may only generate discussion themes, never marriage predictions, loyalty judgments or abuse assessments.

## Safety
Do not infer toxicity, infidelity, possessiveness, narcissism, attachment disorder or future separation from numerology.

## Growth target
Strong interdependence: clear selfhood, mutual influence, shared responsibility and respectful repair.
