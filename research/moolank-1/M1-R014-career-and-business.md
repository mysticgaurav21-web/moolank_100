# M1-R014 — Career and Business Deep Dive

## Metadata
- Record ID: M1-R014
- Status: Research complete; canonical extraction approved

## Scope
Traditional Number 1 themes are translated into work-environment preferences, not fixed professions or success predictions.

## Dimensions
Autonomy, ownership, initiative, innovation, leadership, accountability, collaboration, career identity, entrepreneurship, risk, feedback and sustainable performance.

## Balanced expression
May engage well where goals are clear, ownership is real, initiative is useful, decisions have feedback loops, and contribution is visible. Can start projects, accept responsibility, improve systems and advocate for direction.

## Under pressure
May resist supervision reflexively, over-identify with title, centralize decisions, move before alignment, become impatient with process, or treat career setbacks as personal defeat.

## Underexpressed pattern
May avoid applying for responsibility, wait for permission, hide ideas, tolerate poor role fit, or abandon entrepreneurial interest due to fear of visible failure.

## App guidance
Recommend experiments rather than prescriptions: lead a small project, negotiate decision rights, test an idea, request feedback, document outcomes, or explore roles with appropriate autonomy.

## Business risk boundary
No promise of entrepreneurship, profit, promotion, authority or industry fit. Business, legal and financial decisions require evidence and qualified advice.

## Growth target
Responsible ownership: initiative linked to customer value, ethics, collaboration, feedback and recovery.
