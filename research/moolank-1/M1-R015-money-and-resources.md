# M1-R015 — Money and Resources Deep Dive

## Metadata
- Record ID: M1-R015
- Status: Research complete; canonical extraction approved

## Dimensions
Financial independence, earning motivation, spending, saving, negotiation, ownership, status consumption, risk, generosity, setbacks and long-term security.

## Balanced expression
Uses money as a tool for autonomy and meaningful goals; tracks cash flow; separates confidence from risk evidence; negotiates clearly; saves for resilience; and shares resources without using money for control.

## Under pressure
May take concentrated risks to recover status, overspend on visible success, reject advice, make financial outcomes part of identity, or conceal losses.

## Underexpressed pattern
May avoid financial decisions, underprice work, defer control to others without oversight, or fear investment and negotiation despite adequate information.

## Protective factors
Written budgets, emergency reserves, diversification, cooling-off periods, independent review, maximum-loss limits, documented agreements and regular financial check-ins.

## Safety
Numerology must never produce investment picks, gambling guidance, debt decisions, tax/legal advice, guaranteed wealth or lucky-money predictions.

## App prompts
- Is this purchase useful, symbolic or status-protective?
- What is the maximum acceptable loss?
- Which assumption needs independent verification?
- Does this decision improve resilience or only appearance?

## Growth target
Resource stewardship: autonomy supported by evidence, restraint, transparency and long-term resilience.
