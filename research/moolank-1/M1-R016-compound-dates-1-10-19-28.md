## Canonical rule

## Variant policy
- **1:** direct single-number form; use only the shared Moolank 1 foundation unless a cited tradition explicitly distinguishes it.
- **10:** retain `10 → 1`; zero must not be treated as scientifically amplifying, protecting or completing the number.
- **19:** retain `19 → 10 → 1`; karmic-debt, victory, destiny and past-life claims are restricted tradition metadata, never factual app conclusions.
- **28:** retain `28 → 10 → 1`; interpretations involving partnership, loss, betrayal or financial reversal are conflicting traditional claims and prohibited as predictions.

## App behavior

1. be labelled traditional;
2. use conditional language;
3. identify source family;
4. avoid fear, fate and event prediction;
5. never override questionnaire evidence.
