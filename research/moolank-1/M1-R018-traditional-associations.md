# M1-R018 — Traditional Associations

## Metadata
- Record ID: M1-R018
- Status: Research complete; canonical extraction approved

## Purpose
Store cultural and numerological correspondences without presenting them as causal or scientifically validated.

## Common traditional associations
- number: 1
- date series: 1, 10, 19, 28
- planetary correspondence: Sun
- symbolic themes: beginning, individuality, visibility, authority, creation, centrality
- frequently proposed day: Sunday
- frequently proposed colours: gold, yellow, orange or solar tones
- frequently proposed materials/gemstones: highly variable by source family

## Evidence classification
- Date-series membership: direct system rule.
- Sun correspondence: recurring traditional attribution.
- Colours, weekdays, directions, gemstones and favourable dates: culturally variable symbolic claims.
- Health, cure, luck, event and destiny claims: restricted or prohibited.

## App display rules
Every association must include `classification: tradition-only`, source family, confidence in cross-source consistency, and a disclaimer that no causal effect is established.

## Allowed use
Optional aesthetic themes, journaling prompts, cultural education and user-selected rituals that are safe and non-exclusive.

## Restricted use
Do not tell users to delay important decisions for a lucky day, buy costly gemstones, replace treatment, or expect protection, wealth or authority.

## Prohibited claims
Disease vulnerability, cure, accident prevention, guaranteed luck, supernatural protection, fertility, lifespan, legal success or financial gain.

## Canonical conclusion
The Sun association may be stored as traditional metadata. It must not drive personality certainty, health advice, compatibility scores or future predictions.
