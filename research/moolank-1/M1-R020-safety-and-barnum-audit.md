# M1-R020 — Safety and Barnum-Effect Audit

## Metadata
- Record ID: M1-R020
- Status: Audit complete

## Required safeguards
1. Label numerology as a traditional reflective framework.
2. Keep scientific birth-number link `not-established`.
3. Use may/can/in some contexts rather than fixed identity language.
4. Include balanced, overextended and underexpressed possibilities.
5. Include triggers and protective factors for every shadow.
6. Ask behaviour-specific questions and allow user disagreement.
7. Never diagnose, predict certainty or assign moral worth.
8. Never use fear, urgency or costly remedies to increase engagement.

## Prohibited domains
Medical diagnosis/treatment, psychiatric labels, criminality, addiction, abuse inference, fertility, lifespan, death, accident, legal outcome, guaranteed wealth, investment advice, marriage certainty, infidelity and supernatural protection.

## Barnum-risk patterns
- flattering statements applicable to nearly everyone;
- paired opposites without conditions;
- vague claims that cannot be checked;
- selective confirmation prompts;
- hiding contradictions;
- presenting user agreement as validation of numerology.

## Audit questions
- Is the statement behaviourally specific?
- Can the user reject it without being told resistance proves it?
- Is context included?
- Is a counter-pattern represented?
- Could this claim cause financial, medical, relational or psychological harm?
- Does the output distinguish tradition from evidence?

## Approved disclaimer
“Moolank interpretations are traditional reflection prompts, not scientific personality tests or predictions. Keep what is useful, reject what does not fit, and base important decisions on evidence, context and qualified advice.”

## Audit result
Moolank 1 foundation is eligible for canonical consolidation only when all final JSON fields preserve these safeguards.
